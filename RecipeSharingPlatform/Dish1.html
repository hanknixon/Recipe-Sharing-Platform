Hamburger

A hamburger is a popular sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat, typically beef, placed inside a sliced bread roll or bun. It is often garnished with various toppings such as lettuce, tomatoes, onions, pickles, and condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder (optional)
  • Onion powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Shape the ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patties on a grill or in a skillet until desired doneness.
  3. Toast the burger buns if desired.
  4. Assemble the hamburger with your choice of toppings.

Prep and Cooking Time

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Extras

You can add bacon, fried eggs, or avocado slices for extra flavor.

Special Note

Hamburgers are best served hot and fresh, and are commonly paired with fries or onion rings.
